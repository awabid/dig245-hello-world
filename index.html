I completed multiple learn-computing activities. Projects I'm interested in

1. Hate with Friends

Link

This site lets you connect to Facebook and choose friends you hate, and discover the ones who hate you back. I love this because of the way it manipulates a social tool like Facebook and demonstrates how the same features that it uses for positivity can be easily turned into less pleasant tools.

2. Species in Pieces


Link

This site illustrates endangered species using beautiful, interactive piece-based web art. Not only is it visually stunning, using just 30 vectors to create images, it offers a dark look at the reality of endangered animals. It offers an engaging way to educate people about an important topic.

3. The Wiki Game

Link

This is a quirky game that gives you a wikipedia article, and a goal article, which you must reach by navigating the links in the given article. Its remarkable how this game pushes you to make connections between two vastly different concepts and figure out how they could be linked. In a sense, it almost makes you think like an AI.
